# Buymoria Research Hub

… WE DO MORE

Buymoria Research Hub is a data and research analysis initiative providing professional support across the full research lifecycle — from study design and data collection to analysis, reporting, and evidence packaging.

## About

Buymoria Research Hub supports:

- Public health research projects
- Monitoring & Evaluation (M&E) systems
- Academic research (Undergraduate, MSc, PhD)
- Development sector programmes
- NGO and institutional data needs
- Field research operations

Our focus is transforming raw data into accurate, structured, and decision-ready evidence.

---

## Core Services

### 📊 Quantitative Data Analysis
- Data cleaning and validation
- Descriptive and inferential statistics
- Survey analysis
- Cross-tabulations
- Report-ready tables and charts
- Tools: Stata, IBM SPSS, Excel

### 🧠 Qualitative Data Analysis
- FGD / KII / IDI transcription
- Coding and thematic analysis
- Theme matrix development
- Narrative reporting

### 📋 Monitoring & Evaluation
- Indicator tracking
- M&E framework support
- Monthly reporting templates
- Data quality reviews
- Evaluation-ready documentation

### 🗂 Data Management & Digital Systems
- CRF/eCRF workflows
- REDCap-style systems
- KoboToolbox, ODK, SurveyCTO
- Data dictionaries
- Archive management
